UX RESEARCHER AND UX DESIGNER

I'm an UX Researcher and UX Designer who loves to code. My goal is to research and develop user experiences and user interfaces that take into account human factors research on how humans process information to produce delightful experiences. I have previous research experience in Neuroscience, Psychology, and Visual Perception. I'm published in The Journal of Neuroscience.
